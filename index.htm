Who We Are

Africa Software Architects (ASA), is an IT technology and digital transformation company that seeks to offer software solutions that will disrupt and automate the way organizations do business. The business firm is based in Sandton, South Africa. Our business helps to foster digital transformation to clients

Africa Software Architects, was founded in 2018. We seek to offer our clients industry disruptive solutions which helps them grow their profitability, clientele base and their global reach. We help our clients in the specialty areas of software development, software architecture, web development, mobile app development, Artificial Intelligence, Robotics Process Automation, DevOps solutions, agile project management and business intelligence solutions.
